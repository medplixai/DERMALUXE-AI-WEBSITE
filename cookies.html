1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site work, remember your preferences, and provide information to the site owners. Similar technologies include local storage and pixels.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • Essential — required for the Site to function and to remember your cookie-consent choice.
  • Analytics — help us understand how visitors use the Site so we can improve it.
  • Marketing — used to measure and improve our campaigns and, where applicable, show relevant content.

3. Types of Cookies We May Use

Strictly necessary

Enable core functionality such as page navigation and storing your consent preference. The Site cannot function properly without these.

Performance & analytics

Collect anonymous, aggregated information about how the Site is used (for example via analytics tools) to help us improve performance and content.

Functional

Remember choices you make to provide a more personalised experience.

Marketing / third-party

Set by us or third parties (such as social or advertising platforms) to deliver and measure communications.

4. Managing Your Cookies

When you first visit the Site, our cookie banner lets you Accept or Decline non-essential cookies. You can change your choice at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this Site, which will make the banner appear again.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings — most browsers let you block or delete cookies. Note that disabling some cookies may affect how the Site functions.

Some features may rely on third-party services (for example WhatsApp, Instagram, Google Maps or analytics providers) that may set their own cookies. We do not control these cookies; please refer to the respective providers' policies.
